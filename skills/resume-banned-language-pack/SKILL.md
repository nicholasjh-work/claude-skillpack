# Resume Banned Language Pack

Apply this pack when writing or editing resumes.

## Hard bans from the user's standing preferences
Do not use:
- Orchestrated
- Championed
- Pioneered
- Helmed
- Realm
- Comprehensive
- Demonstrating
- Showcasing
- Measurable
- Meticulous
- Proven ability
- Proven record
- Approximately
- the intersection of

## Additional resume quality rules
- Do not write generic executive-summary filler.
- Do not invent metrics, scope, headcount, budgets, tools, or business impact.
- Do not use "results-driven", "dynamic professional", "seasoned leader", or similar empty labels.
- Prefer real systems, real actions, real constraints, and real outcomes.
- Every bullet should answer: what changed, how it changed, and why it mattered.
- Keep verbs plain and credible.
